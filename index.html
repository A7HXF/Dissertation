Why is cybersecurity crucial for seniors?

As technology evolves, so do the risks associated with online activities.

Cybercriminals often target seniors, taking advantage of their trust and less familiarity with digital threats.

At WiseNet, we believe that cybersecurity is not just about protecting devices; it's about safeguarding your peace of mind. Imagine navigating the internet with confidence, knowing you have the tools to recognize and thwart potential threats.

By understanding the importance of cybersecurity, seniors can:
- Protect personal and financial information.
- Safely connect with friends and family online.
- Enjoy the benefits of technology without fear or hesitation.

Join us on this educational journey to empower yourself with the knowledge needed to embrace the digital world securely. Together, we can create a safer online environment for every senior.

Important Terminologies

Cyber Security

It involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access or damage

Cyber Threats

Refers to potential risks or dangers in the digital realm, such as malware or hacking attempts

Cyber Attack

It is an intentional act that aims to exploit vulnerabilities in these systems, often causing disruption or unauthorized access for malicious purposes.
